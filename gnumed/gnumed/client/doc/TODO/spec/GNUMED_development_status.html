Status definitions:
  • draft: just some ideas, first tries may be reside in test-area, not part of gnumed yet
  • alpha: first implementation, not completely functional, almost untested
  • pre-beta: advanced implementation, almost functional, developer-tested
  • beta: supposed to be functional, excessively developer-tested.
  • final (release quality): tested by a large number of users, supposed to contain no severe bugs
Maintainers: if no known maintainer exists, ask the administrators (horst and ncq)
